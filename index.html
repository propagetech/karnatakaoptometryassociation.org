Who is an Optometrist?

Optometry is a healthcare profession that is autonomous, educated, and regulated (licensed/registered), and optometrists are the primary healthcare practitioners of the eye and visual system who provide comprehensive eye and vision care, which includes refraction and dispensing, detection/diagnosis and management of disease in the eye, and the rehabilitation of conditions of the visual system.
- BY WHO

Our Vision

To work for the upliftment and promotion of profession of optometry and eye care

Our Mission

By providing continuous education through conference, seminars periodically and promotion of Optometry through public awareness

Objectives

  • To ensure and promote interest of its members
  • To start and carry publications of journals, articles, books and other materials.
  • Association also negotiates optometrists' fees represent adequate remuneration for the services provided.
  • Will act as a job center for the members.
  • It will help the members to continue professional education
  • The Association produces a online resource for practice management concerns
  • Association will conduct annual campaigns such as UV Protection, Children's Vision, Good Vision for Life, World Optometry Day and many others.
  • Will try to maintain ongoing relations with other health care fields.
  • The association will publish and supply its members with a range of regular  publications
